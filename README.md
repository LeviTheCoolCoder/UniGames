# UniGames
## Introduction:
UniGames is a website that uses UniBucks, digital money that is aqquired by exchanging USD, to get games. <i> See below for details on money exchanging. </i>  </p>
## Money Exchanging:
We have a wide collection of fun games that we want you to play! How do you play them? We accept UniBucks, a type of digital money to get games on UniGames. You can get UniBucks by exchanging USD at <i>Veritas Elementary School.</i> with one of our managers. The UniBucks will then be transferred to your account.
